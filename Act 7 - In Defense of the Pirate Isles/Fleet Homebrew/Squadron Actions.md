#### Boarding
Can only be attempted if engaged to the target squadron.

**Critical Success** As a success, but the attacker decides how to distribute damage to the opposing squadron's ships.
**Success** Deal damage equivalent to the squadron's damage stat.

#### Fire the Cannons
Can only be attempted if at near distance to the target squadron.

**Critical Success** As a success, but the attacker decides how to distribute damage to the opposing squadron's ships.
**Success** Deal damage equivalent to the squadron's damage stat.

#### Sail
The army attempts to close or expand the distance between itself and one or more squadrons. The commodore attempts a maneuver check against the maneuver DC of the enemy squadron it seeks to engage with, or against the highest maneuver DC of the enemy squadrons it attempts to disengage with, taking a -2 penalty per engaged squadron.

**Success** Become near if engaged, or engaged if near.
**Failure** Your squadron's attempt to maneuver fails.
**Critical Failure** Your squadron becomes disorganized and becomes mired 1 (frightened1)

#### Defensive Tactics
The squadron gains a +2 bonus to its armor class against a specific enemy squadron until the start of its next turn.

#### Retreat (3 Actions)
Your squadron becomes distant. If it is already distant it flees the battlefield.





#### Reckless Maneuver
The squadron gains a +4 bonus to attack rolls, but suffers a -2 bonus to armor class. This bonus can not stack with the bonus from **Assault Tactics**.


#### Assault Tactics
The squadron gains a +2 bonus to its attack value, and the next time it deals damage to a squadron the attacking commodore decides how to distribute damage.
