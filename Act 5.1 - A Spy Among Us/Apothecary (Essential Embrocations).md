#### General
Decaying grandeur of an old manor. Walls made of clay brick. Chimney pokes out of the roof with a 1 sqft opening.

**Schedule**
Three guards in the guard room during the day. Two in the hallway at day and night. They patrol when shifts switch every 2 hours. The hallway door is locked at night.

**Guard Tactics**
Attempt to fight in open areas with superior numbers. Attempt to fight in narrow corridors when outnumbered. Call for quarter when low on health.

**Gather Information**
- Location
- Its been closed for a little while
- It's said that they sell poison and drugs there as well as poultices and medicinal herbs
- They've been selling at a steal recently to people they allow in
