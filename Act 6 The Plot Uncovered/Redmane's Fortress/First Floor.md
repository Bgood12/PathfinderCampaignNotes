#### Kitchen
Industrial hearth with meat racks suspended from the ceiling above. The room constantly smells of smoked meats and sea salt.


#### Garden
The garden is choked with bramble thick hedges, has non-native flora such as red orchids and nettles. Sometimes he has crewmen weed the garden barefoot as a punishment
