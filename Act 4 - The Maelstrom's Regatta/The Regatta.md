Mechanics:
- Scoring
	- Succeeding on a check to advance in the race earns one Victory Point
	- Critically succeeding earns two points
	- Critically failing loses one point
- Roll on the [[Maelstrom Hazards]] table for each leg of the race
	- Don't roll at race start
	- Roll 3 times for into the storm

A sharp wind picks up and begins pushing the milling ships around as they raise anchor (DC25 Sailing Lore, +2DC for other reasonable skills).
	- Critical failure results in a collision 
		- 50% [[The Howling Jib]] hits another ship, thus taking 3d10 damage (DT15)
		- 50% another ship hits [[The Howling Jib]], thus dealing 6d10 damage


**Sandbar**
- Sail around the sandbar 
	- Lose 5 Victory Points
- 4 successes required for successful navigation
	- DC25 Survival to navigate
	- DC25 Perception with a swift aquatic or aerial scout
	- Critical failure loses 2 Victory Points as the ship runs aground
		- If there's a scout lose only one victory point as usual
	- Failure loses 1 Victory Point as the ship needs to backtrack
- Each successful navigation check can be paired with a DC25 Sailing Lore check for speed


**The Whispering Winds**
Region with unpredictable air and sea currents
	- Strong and unpredictable air and sea currents shift erratically in this area of the sea
- 3 successes navigate through the currents without losing time due to becoming lost or dragged back
	- DC25 Nature check, or DC27 Survival check to navigate and rapidly adjust the sails
- Each successful navigation check can be paired with a DC20 Sailing Lore check for speed


**Sea Monster**
As [[The Howling Jib]] approaches the PCs see a ship (The Smiling Kraken) being capsized by a large chelonian shadow.
- DC25 Sailing Lore to outmaneuver the turtle
	- Failure begins combat, above check can be repeated once per round to escape
- Check is reduced to DC15 if the PCs wait for another ship to catch the turtle's attention
	- Lose 1d4 Victory Points 

Dragon Turtle: https://2e.aonprd.com/Monsters.aspx?ID=2956


**Brush with Storm**
The part of the race that takes place within the maelstrom itself
- 5 DC30 Sailing Lore checks 
	- Successes in crease Victory Points as usual
	- Critically Failing 
		- Ship takes 4d12 electricity damage as if hit by the Lightning Bolt spell
			- Lightning Bolt: https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1586
			- The lightning tears and inflames the sails if this brings the ship below half hit points (broken threshold)
		- If the ship is below half hit points
			- Sheer winds rip through the patchwork repairs made to the sails
	- Repairing the sails 
		- Climb to them DC20 athletics (DC16 with climb speed)
		- Jury rig sails DC20 craft check


**Elemental Attack**
- A trio of Living Whirlwinds form as a result of the power of the storm.
	- 1 action must be spent steering the ship each round
		- Failing to do this will cost 1 Victory Point as the ship is uncontroled
Elite Living Whirlwind statblock: https://2e.aonprd.com/Monsters.aspx?ID=2974&Elite=true&NoRedirect=1

- The firstmate summons an Erinyes to attack the Jibblet's pilot, hoping to slow them down
