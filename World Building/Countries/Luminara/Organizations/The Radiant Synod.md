The Radiant Synod is the Church organization that governs [[Luminara]] and claims spiritual authority far beyond the nation's borders. The Church's doctrine emphasizes order, obedience to divine hierarchy, and the necessity of Church mediation between mortals and gods.

#### Structure
- **The Exarch**: Supreme leader, both spiritual and temporal authority
- **Arch-Prelates**: Govern provinces and oversee major Church functions
- **Prelates**: Administer cities, towns, and large temples
- **Priests**: Conduct services, perform rituals, manage local parishes
- **Wardens**: Church enforcers, handle inquisition and daemon containment
- **Exorcists**: Specialized Wardens trained in combating extraplanar threats
