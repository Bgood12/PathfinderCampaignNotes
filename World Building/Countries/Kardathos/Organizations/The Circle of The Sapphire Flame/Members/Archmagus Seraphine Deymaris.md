Seraphine Deymaris is the guild master of [[The Circle of the Sapphire Flame]].

- She is stern, immaculate, and politically savvy.
- She takes great shame at the current undead state of [[Prince Osric Wystan]]
	- Her involvement had cemented her own authority however.
	- Wants to shut down public talk of the event
		- More likely to aid privately - to set things right

**Appearance**
Her face has light lines of middle age. Her hair is black with silvery lines, held in braids with sapphire pins. She wears deep indigo robes with a silver trim. She has piercing blue eyes, sharp as glass.
