Former colony of [[Veriduna]] .

Balaika is a verdant and diverse country situated in [[Zelvahar]] on the western coast [[Alkebulan]], known for its bustling trade and persistent struggle for self determination. The nation stretches along a picturesque coastline, adorned with beautiful beaches, dense rainforests, and expansive savannas. The geographical diversity lends itself to a wide array of natural resources and a climate that is both bountiful and temperate.

### Cities and Settlements
**Aurelion:** The capital city on the coast. Center of colonial administration and frequent site of protests against colonial rule.
**Marulas:** Major port city serving as a hub for maritime trade. Known for its spice markets and diverse shipping traffic. Also a center of economic inequality and political discontent.
**Savara Springs:** City at the edge of the rainforest, famous for natural springs believed to have healing properties. Attracts tourists and pilgrims. Potential organizing point for resistance movements.

### Culture
Balaika is culturally diverse, comprising indigenous tribes native to the land, descendants of Veridunan colonists, and refugees from various nations. Each group maintains distinct traditions, languages, and festivals.

Art serves as a vehicle for resistance among the native population. Intricate masks, vibrant textiles, and rhythmic drumming often carry coded messages of dissent. Communal meals and shared traditions help preserve indigenous heritage and strengthen bonds against colonial rule.

### Economy
Balaika's economy is driven by its natural resources and strategic coastal position. The nation exports precious minerals, exotic woods, and valuable herbs from its rainforests. Trade flows through its major ports, attracting merchants from neighboring regions. However, the substantial tribute owed to the Pirate Confederacy limits available funds for infrastructure and governance.

### History
Balaika was a colony of [[Veriduna]] that declared independence during the [[Veridunan Civil War]]. Following the war, [[House Kasa]] sought to reclaim the colony as part of rebuilding Imperial [[Veriduna]]. Unable to defend against Veriduna's navy, Balaika made a deal with the [[Pirate Confederacy]]: tribute in exchange for naval protection. This agreement gave rise to [[The First Pirate King]], who led the combined pirate armada against Veriduna's fleet and secured Balaikan independence.

### Government
Balaika's government is dominated by a colonial minority descended from [[Veriduna|Veridunan]] settlers. The tribute paid to the [[Pirate Confederacy]] strains the nation's finances, making it difficult for the colonial government to maintain control over the native population. Political tensions between the colonial government and native inhabitants remain high.
### Political Unrest
The high cost of the tribute, protection money according to some pirates, paid by Balaika to the [[Pirate Confederacy]] leaves the former colonial nation's coffers light. This makes it difficult for the colonial minority to keep control over the native inhabitants of the area.
